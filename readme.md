# Roll dice game

Try to have fun with your friend or yourself. A simple game from our childhood.

## Roll the dice

First player is always on the left side. Click Roll the dice button fot that. You can roll dice until you got 0. Then, it's turn of the second player to roll.

## Save score

You can anytime save a current score by clicking Save score button. It will be added to your global score.

## The first player who reach 101 scores is won

You will see an appropriate message on the screen.

## Replay

You can start over by clicking Replay button.
